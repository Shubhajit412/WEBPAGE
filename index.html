Declarations page
Currently on a sabbatical, I recently served as a Teaching Fellow (Head) in the Computer Science department at Ashoka University, New Delhi and had completed my BS-MS dual degree in Mathematics at IISER Bhopal with a minor in Data Science & Engineering, in 2024.

My research interests revolve around approximation theory, numerical analysis on eigenvalue problems and optimization theory on continuous domains. Recently, in the dawn of active research in deep learning, I have started to devote my attention towards proceeding of works on the usage of Physics-Informed Neural Networks (PINNs) in function approximations.

Beyond my formal academic trajectory, I find myself intellectually drawn towards the quieter intersections of anthropology, jurisprudence and behavioural finance.

Please have a look around this website, to know more about me and the little dent I am trying to put in the academic universe!
