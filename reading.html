The Paper Tree

In a modest attempt to expand the span of my existing intellectual orbit, I have always tried to go through a well-diversified spectrum of voices since the beginning of this ever expanding reading expedition. The literary voyage since appears to be a bit more sparse than what I anticipated it to be. Below is the complete list of all the titles I have read so far, updated through February 2026. The count presently stands at 121 titles, of which 61 are non-fiction & 87 are paperbacks.
For the record, most of these books were procured from various branches of Bahrisons Booksellers, Crosswords and independent local bookstores across the cities I have had the privilege of residing in.
